In my childhood I was moved by a poem of our national poet Kazi Nazrul Islam where a child tries to know the reasons of virtually everything. This poem inspired me trying to know the logic of how the world around us is working and how talented people are inventing new things which change the face of the world radically. I apply my knowledge of Physics to understand the cause of the behavior of condensed matters in atomic level.
